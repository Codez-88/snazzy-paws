Owner Bios

Jane – Founder & Grooming Specialist

With 12 years of experience, Jane founded Snazzy Paws Grooming to create a warm, welcoming space for dogs and owners. Known for her gentle touch and expert styling, she treats every pup like family.

Sarah – Senior Pet Stylist

Sarah brings a creative flair to every cut and style. With a background in pet fashion and behavior, she has a special talent for helping nervous dogs feel at ease during their transformation.

Mike – Puppy Care Coordinator

Mike is our go-to guy for wrangling the littlest clients. His calm demeanor and playful energy make him a puppy favorite. He ensures each pup’s first grooming experience is a positive one.

Lisa – Spa & Bath Expert

Lisa is the heart behind our spa treatments. From luxurious baths to soothing massages, she pampers every pooch with care. Her specialty? Making every dog leave smelling fresh and feeling fabulous.
